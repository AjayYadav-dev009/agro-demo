Our Story

Agrotonomy was born out of a simple idea – to make fresh, healthy food accessible everywhere while using fewer natural resources.

From our early days of building our first tower farms to working on large-scale greenhouse projects, our focus has remained the same: innovation, sustainability and impact.

Today, we're proud to be a global partner in the journey toward a greener future.
